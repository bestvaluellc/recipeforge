Account & Data Deletion Request

RecipeForge by Best Value LLC

If you would like to request deletion of your RecipeForge account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Email us at:

support@bestvaluellc.com

Please include:

  • The email address used to sign in (Google or Apple)
  • Subject line: "Delete Account Request"

We may request additional verification to confirm account ownership before processing deletion.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted

  • Your authentication account will be permanently removed
  • All saved recipes and preferences will be deleted
  • Usage counters and associated app data will be erased
  • Subscription access will be terminated (if applicable)

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days of verification.

Data That May Be Retained

We may retain limited information if required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Financial recordkeeping related to purchases
  • Fraud prevention or security purposes

Any retained data will be limited to what is legally required.
