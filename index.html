Frequently Asked Questions

How many free recipes do I get daily?

You get 10 free AI-crafted recipes each day. Usage resets at local midnight.

Do I need an account?

Yes, sign in with Apple or Google to sync usage and access purchases across devices.

What does Full Access include?

Unlimited recipes and an ad-free experience.

My purchase didn’t unlock Full Access.

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. If it doesn’t unlock, contact support with your Apple receipt.

Does RecipeForge work offline?

Recipe generation requires an internet connection.
