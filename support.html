RecipeForge Support

Here you’ll find answers to common questions and ways to get help with the app.

How do I generate a recipe?

Choose “Discover a Recipe,” select your ingredients, and tap Generate Recipe. You’ll get a complete recipe with steps, timing, cuisine style, and a food photo (if available for your plan).

Why am I seeing a daily limit?

RecipeForge includes free usage limits to help keep things sustainable:

  • Guest users (no account): 1 free recipe generation per day, no AI photos.
  • Signed-in free users (Apple / Google): up to 5 free recipe generations per day, plus a limited number of free AI food photos.

Upgrading to a paid plan removes most limits and unlocks full AI photo support for every recipe.

Are the food photos real?

No — they are high-quality AI-generated images based on your recipe ingredients and cuisine type.

How do I save a recipe?

Tap the Save button on any recipe page to store it in your personal AI Cookbook.

Does RecipeForge work without an account?

Yes — you can use RecipeForge for free without creating an account, or you can sign in for extra benefits. There are two free paths:

1. Guest user (no account required)

  • No login needed.
  • Recipes are stored directly on your device.
  • You can generate 1 recipe per day.
  • AI food photos are not available for guest users.

2. Signed-in free user (Apple / Google login)

  • Recipes can sync across your devices and are easier to restore if you reinstall.
  • You can generate up to 5 recipes per day.
  • You get a lifetime total of 3 free AI food photos.
  • You can watch a reward video to earn an additional AI photo when available.

Upgrading to a paid plan unlocks more usage, removes most limits, and enables AI photos for every recipe.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

You can report any issues directly to the support email below.
